Tianji Liu

Ph.D. Candidate

Department of Computer Science and Engineering
The Chinese University of Hong Kong

tjliu [at] cse.cuhk.edu.hk

Tianji Liu is currently a Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong supervised by Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young. He graduated with M.Sc. from University College London in 2020 and B.Eng. from Tsinghua University in 2019. His research interests are parallel and efficient algorithms for logic synthesis and verification.
